# 🍫 Chocolate Sales Analysis

## 📌 Overview

This project analyzes chocolate sales data to uncover sales trends, product performance, and regional sales insights. The analysis aims to identify patterns that can support data-driven business decisions.

## 🗂️ Dataset

The Chocolate Sales Dataset contains transaction-level sales data, including:
- Sales Person
- Country
- Product
- Date
- Amount
- Boxes Shipped

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Jupyter Notebook

## 📊 Analysis Performed

- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Data type conversion
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Sales trend analysis
- Product performance analysis
- Country-wise sales analysis
- Sales person performance analysis
- Data visualization

## 📈 Key Insights

- Australia recorded the highest total sales, while New Zealand had the lowest sales during the analysis period.
- Monthly sales showed an overall upward trend from 2022 to 2024. January recorded the highest total revenue, while April had the lowest sales. However, transaction volume peaked in June and reached its lowest point in February, indicating that higher transaction volume does not always result in higher revenue.
- Ches Bonnell generated the highest total revenue, while Kelci Walkden recorded the highest number of transactions. Meanwhile, Madelene Upcott achieved the highest average revenue per transaction, suggesting that sales performance can vary depending on the metrics used.
- Smooth Sliky Salty was the best-selling chocolate product overall, although product preferences varied across countries. For example, 50% Dark Bites was the top-selling product in Australia.

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Optimize inventory allocation based on country-specific purchasing patterns rather than applying a uniform inventory strategy. While Smooth Sliky Salty performed well overall, product priorities should be adjusted based on each country's demand. For example, Australia should prioritize 50% Dark Bites to better align inventory with local customer preferences.
- Evaluate sales performance using multiple KPIs, including total revenue, transaction volume, and average revenue per transaction. This allows the company to distinguish between sales representatives who generate revenue through higher sales volume and those who achieve higher-value transactions, supporting more effective performance evaluation and targeted sales strategies.
- Monitor both revenue and transaction trends when evaluating monthly performance. Understanding the difference between sales volume and sales value can help identify whether changes in revenue are driven by more transactions or higher-value purchases.
